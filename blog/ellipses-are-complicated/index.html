Ellipses are complicated#

Ellipses. Just squashed circles, right? Well, maybe. Though it’s more correct to call circles stretched out ellipses.

Ellipses are but one of three types of conic section, among parabolas and hyperbolas, and a circle is a special case of ellipse (in which the focal points are the same).
